About NeuroScan AI

Our advanced AI-powered system helps medical professionals detect and classify brain tumors with high accuracy from MRI scans, enabling early intervention and better patient outcomes.

Early Detection

Our system uses deep learning models to detect brain tumors in early stages from MRI scans, helping to save lives through timely diagnosis.

Tumor Classification

The system classifies tumor types including glioma, meningioma, and pituitary tumors, aiding doctors in choosing the right treatment approach.

MRI Image Analysis

Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) models are used to analyze MRI scan images for accurate identification of abnormal brain tissues.

Rapid Results

Get AI-powered analysis results within seconds, enabling faster clinical decision-making without lengthy waiting times.

High Accuracy

Our models are trained on thousands of MRI scans to ensure reliable predictions with minimal false positives and negatives.

User-Friendly Interface
